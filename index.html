⚡ Features

Everything you need
on the bench

Designed by handball coaches, for handball coaches. Every feature built to save time during the match and deliver clear insights after.

🎯

Shot Tracking by Zone

Record goals and saves for 7 different shot zones: 6m, 9m, penalty, wing, pivot, 1st and 2nd phase. Automatic efficiency percentages.

🧤

Goalkeeper Shot Maps

Interactive shot maps for each goalkeeper. Tap on the goal to mark exactly where shots landed. Independent maps per GK.

📊

Complete Match Summary

Team stats, shot efficiency, ball management, fouls and individual player tables — all in one clear overview.

📄

Export & Share Reports

Professional two-page layout ready for print: team statistics on page 1, individual player tables on page 2. Share via email, WhatsApp or Drive.

📈

Goal Timeline

Visual timeline showing the flow of the match — see when goals were scored by each team at a glance.

🏆

Season Archive

Full match history with cumulative season stats, win/draw/loss record, goals scored and conceded. Backup and restore anytime.
