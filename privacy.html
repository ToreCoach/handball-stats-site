Privacy Policy

Handball Match Stats · Last updated: May 15, 2026

✓ No data sold to third parties

This Privacy Policy describes how Handball Match Stats ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

Handball Match Stats collects only the data necessary to provide the service:

  • Account data: email address and password (used for authentication via Firebase)
  • App data: match statistics, team and player information, and seasonal archives that you enter manually

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your account
  • To store and synchronize your match statistics across devices
  • To allow you to export reports in PDF and Excel format

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which complies with GDPR and other applicable privacy regulations. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is used exclusively to provide the functionality of the app.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

7. Children's Privacy

Handball Match Stats is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
